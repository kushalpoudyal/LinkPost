Privacy Policy

Your data, your rights. We protect your privacy at every step.

1. Data Collection

We collect only the information needed to provide our services:

  • Name, contact details, and address (for delivery and support)
  • Parcel and transaction details (for tracking and billing)
  • Usage data (site analytics, device/browser info)

2. Data Usage

Your data is used to:

  • Process and deliver your shipments
  • Provide tracking, notifications, and customer support
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and access controls to protect your data. Only authorized staff can access personal information, and all access is logged.

4. User Rights (GDPR Basics)

  • Access: You can request a copy of your data at any time.
  • Correction: Update your information via your profile or by contacting support.
  • Deletion: Request account deletion and data erasure at any time.
  • Restriction: Ask us to limit processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Objection: You may object to certain uses of your data (e.g., marketing).

5. Consent & Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing. Data is only shared with delivery partners as required to fulfill your shipment, and only with your consent.

You may withdraw consent at any time. We will never sell your data.

6. Contact & Questions

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, email privacy@linkpost.com.

Last updated: October 2025. This policy may be updated as regulations or services change.
