About Us

QuickShip Logistics powering Link Post – reliable, sustainable, inclusive parcel delivery.

Our Story

QuickShip Logistics began as a small regional courier pilot in 2022, focused on fixing late pickups and opaque tracking across Australian metro corridors. By integrating real‑time scan events, route batching and carbon‑aware lane selection, the platform evolved into today’s Link Post experience.

In 2023 we introduced eco consolidation and bulk merchant tools. Through 2024–2025 we expanded interstate line‑haul partners, lifted on‑time performance, and reduced average packaging waste per parcel. Our mission: make fast delivery and sustainability complementary, not competing goals.

Now Link Post connects senders and receivers nationwide—with transparency, care and measurable impact.
